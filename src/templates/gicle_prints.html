Giclée Fine Art Prints

PRINTING

Archival pigment printing, EPSON UltraChrome HDX Wide Gamut Inks, 1440 X 2880 DPI printer resolution.

PAPER

EPSON Hot Press Bright, acid free, 100% cotton rag media with a bright smooth matte finish, 17 mil thickness. Print images are 18”W x 32”H with a ½ inch white border. Paper is cut to 19”W x 33”H. Prints are delivered in an acid-free clear sleeve and backing board.

FRAMING

There are a number of framing and matting options ranging in price available through the internet and local framing shops. Please email info@BrigLightArt.com if you need further assistance finding the right framing option for your needs.
